Research Domain

AI-driven social integration & wellbeing for university freshers — bridging emotional support, peer connection, and proactive risk detection.

Literature Survey

University freshers face emotional, social, and academic challenges during their transition to higher education.

38%Experience anxiety
34%Report depression
65%Social isolation

Traditional support systems like counseling and mentoring are often reactive and limited.

Recent AI tools focus mainly on academic performance or mental health, rarely integrating emotional wellbeing, social engagement, and peer relationships. Research highlights peer connections as key to reducing dropout rates, but current systems remain fragmented .

Research Gap

Existing AI solutions have critical gaps:

  • Emotional and social neglect – Focus on academics only
  • Standalone tools – Lack integration and holistic support
  • Static profiling – No dynamic student understanding
  • No continuous monitoring – Limited real-time risk detection
Fragmented systems Static profiling No real-time adaptation

Research Problem

Freshers struggle with:

  • Social integration and loneliness
  • Emotional stress and mental health challenges
  • Academic pressure and disengagement

Current barriers: Support systems are reactive and fragmented, leading to:

⚠️ Loneliness and social disconnection

📉 Disengagement from academics

❌ Mental health deterioration affecting success

Research Objectives

🎯 Main Objective

To design and develop an AI-based platform that supports freshers through social integration, emotional wellbeing, and early risk detection.

📋 Specific Objectives

  • Adaptive student profiling – Understand student dynamics continuously
  • AI-based risk detection – Identify at-risk students early
  • Intelligent peer matching – Foster meaningful connections
  • AI conversational support – Provide empathetic assistance 24/7
  • Platform effectiveness evaluation – Measure real-world impact

Methodology

📊 Data Collection

Data from 120 first-year students via weekly check-ins informed UniPulse development. Structured surveys capture emotional states, stress levels, social interactions, academic comfort, and wellbeing metrics.

🎓 Four Core Modules

  • Adaptive Profiling – Dynamic student understanding
  • Risk Detection – Early warning system
  • Conversational Support – AI-powered assistant
  • Peer Matching – Intelligent connections

🔧 Backend Stack

  • Framework: Laravel 11
  • Database: MySQL
  • Version Control: Git/GitHub

🤖 AI Technologies

  • Machine Learning: Random Forest, K-Means Clustering
  • NLP: RoBERTa
  • LLM Integration: OpenAI API
