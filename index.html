About the Research

Reimagining Student Support with AI

The transition into university life often presents significant academic, social, and emotional challenges for first-year students, leading to isolation, stress, and increased dropout rates. Existing support systems tend to be static and non-personalized, failing to capture the dynamic needs of students.

UniPulse is an AI-driven platform that provides proactive, personalized, and scalable student support through four integrated modules leveraging behavioral analytics and emotion recognition to continuously build student profiles, detect social inclusion risks, facilitate meaningful peer connections, and deliver real-time wellbeing interventions.

Research Modules

Four Integrated AI Modules

Each module targets a distinct dimension of student wellbeing and social inclusion — working together as a cohesive, intelligent support ecosystem.

Module 01

Adaptive Student Profiling & Learning Persona Management

Generates and continuously updates multidimensional student profiles using academic, behavioral, emotional, and social interaction data through weekly check-ins enabling dynamic, deeply personalized support.

Module 02

Intelligent Peer Matching & Relationship Evolution Management

Facilitates meaningful peer connections by computing real-time compatibility scores from mood, stress, and engagement data helping students build lasting support networks and social bonds.

Module 03

Social Inclusion Risk Detection & Early Intervention Management

Detects at-risk students early by integrating behavioral, emotional, and social indicators then activates culturally appropriate, timely interventions before issues escalate.

Module 04

Conversational Support & Student Wellbeing Management

An AI-powered chatbot providing continuous emotional assistance and crisis detection with a three-tier severity scoring system ensuring proportionate, responsible real-time wellbeing support.
